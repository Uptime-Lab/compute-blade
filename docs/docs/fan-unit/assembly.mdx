# Assembly

Fan units will come in four parts
- Fan Unit board
- Left Bracket
- Right Bracket
- 5V Fan v

:::note
    The Fan Unit is designed for a 5V fan like the Noctua [NF-A4x20 5V PWM](https://noctua.at/en/products/fan/nf-a4x20-5v-pwm)
:::

<img src="/img/fan-unit/sta-parts.png" alt="Fan Unit parts" width={'70%'}/>

Start by installing the Brackets onto the Fan. The brackets are toolless and wil snap right on to the fan.

:::note
    The Fan Brackets are not symmetrical. and that the fan is mounted for your desiered air flow configeration
:::

<img src="/img/fan-unit/brackets.png" alt="fans with Bkractest installed" width={'70%'}/>

Next Slied the Fan with the brackets into the Fan Unit board

<img src="/img/fan-unit/fan.png" alt="Fan installed onto fan unit board" width={'70%'}/>


Finaly, connect the Fan to the 4-pin fan header on the fan unit board

<img src="/img/fan-unit/assembled.png" alt="fully assembed Fan Unit" width={'70%'}/>

### Conecting to Compute Blade
:::info
    The Fan Units are designed to work out with the [Blade Runner™️](/blade-runner) enclosers
:::

:::danger
    Pulging the Compute Blade into the Fan Unit incorrectly may lead to permanent damage to the fan unit and the blade
:::

When conecting the blade to the Fan Unit the the borids should be 90° relitive to each other
No addtional work is need to make the Fan unit run.
