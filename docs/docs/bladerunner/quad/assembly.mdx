# Assembly

The Quad BladeRunner™️ toolless allowing the quick replacement of part in the event of a failure. It is able to contain 4 Compute Blades and 2 Fan Units

<img src="/img/bladerunner/quad/tc-parts.webp" alt="Quad BladeRunner with 4 dev Compute Blade and 2 Fan Units" width={'70%'}/>

## Fan Unit

The Quad BladeRunner™️ is designed with the Fan Unit in mind

<img src="/img/bladerunner/quad/tc-back.webp" alt="2 Quad BladeRunner next to each other" width={'70%'}/>

Insert the Fan Unit into the back of the Quad BladeRunner™️ Making sure the fan unit is under the small catch. 
The The fan unit will snap into place with the fan seated just inside the Quad BladeRunner™️

<img src="/img/bladerunner/quad/tc-fan.webp" alt="2 Quad BladeRunner next to each other" width={'70%'}/>

## Compute Blade

The Compute bade are inserted in to the front of the Quad BladeRunner™️, align the blade with the groves

:::warning
If the blade does not slide all the way back, It is possibles for the fan connecter to become misallied. remove the Blade and Fan Unit to check for damage
Then re insert both into the BladeRunner™️
:::

<img src="/img/bladerunner/quad/tc-blade-rt.webp" alt="4 Compute Blades in Quad BladeRunner™️" width={'70%'}/>





## Expanding

Quad BladeRunner™️ can be joined together to create a larger enclosure

:::warning
The tolerances on the Quad BladeRunner™️ use a tight friction fit and a rubber melt be require to assemble and disassemble
:::
